About Dig it Up

Helping you unearth legitimate online earning opportunities since 2024

Our Mission

At Dig it Up, we believe that everyone deserves access to legitimate opportunities to earn extra income online. In a digital landscape cluttered with scams, empty promises, and misleading information, we set out to create a trusted resource that separates the real opportunities from the noise.

Our mission is simple: to help people discover, evaluate, and succeed with legitimate online earning platforms. Whether you're looking to supplement your income with surveys during your commute, build a freelance career, or explore passive income options, we're here to guide you with honest, researched information.

Why We Started

The idea for Dig it Up came from personal experience. Like many people, we spent countless hours researching ways to earn extra money online. We encountered countless "opportunities" that turned out to be scams, wasted time on platforms that never paid, and fell for exaggerated income claims.

We realized that what people really need isn't another list of 100 ways to make money online – they need a carefully curated directory of platforms that have been vetted for legitimacy, reliability, and realistic earning potential. That's what Dig it Up provides.

Our Approach

Every platform featured in our directory goes through a thorough evaluation process:

  • Legitimacy Check: We verify that the company is real, has a track record, and actually pays users as promised.
  • User Research: We analyze reviews, complaints, and feedback from real users across multiple sources.
  • Payment Verification: We confirm payment methods, minimum thresholds, and payout reliability.
  • Realistic Expectations: We provide honest earning estimates based on actual user reports, not marketing hype.

We don't feature platforms that require upfront payments, make unrealistic promises, or have patterns of non-payment. Our reputation depends on yours trust, and we take that responsibility seriously.

What We're Not

To set proper expectations, here's what Dig it Up is NOT:

  • We're not a "get rich quick" site – building income takes time and effort
  • We don't guarantee specific earnings – results depend on individual circumstances
  • We're not affiliated with the platforms we review – we're an independent directory
  • We don't provide financial, legal, or professional advice

Our Values

Honesty: We tell it like it is. If a platform has significant drawbacks, we'll mention them. If earnings are modest, we won't inflate the numbers.

Transparency: We disclose when we may earn commissions from links. This doesn't influence our reviews, but we believe you should know.

User-First: Every decision we make is guided by what's best for our readers. That's why we'll never feature a platform just because it pays high affiliate commissions.

Continuous Improvement: The online earning landscape changes constantly. We regularly update our reviews and remove platforms that no longer meet our standards.

Get in Touch

We love hearing from our readers. Whether you have questions about a platform, suggestions for new additions, or feedback about our site, please don't hesitate to reach out through our contact page.

Thank you for trusting Dig it Up as your guide to online earning. Here's to uncovering your earning potential!
