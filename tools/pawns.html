What is Pawns.app?

Pawns.app combines passive income from bandwidth sharing with optional paid surveys. It's similar to Honeygain but differentiates itself with a lower minimum payout ($5 vs $20) and additional survey opportunities to boost earnings.

How It Works

Passive Earnings: Install the app and share unused internet bandwidth. Runs quietly in the background on Windows, Mac, Linux, Android, and iOS.

Surveys: Complete optional surveys for additional earnings. This active component helps reach payout faster.

Earning Potential

Bandwidth sharing alone earns $15-40/month depending on location and devices. Adding surveys can boost total to $30-60/month. The low $5 minimum means you can cash out relatively quickly.
