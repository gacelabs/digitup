What is Google Opinion Rewards?

Google Opinion Rewards is an official Google app that pays you for answering quick surveys. Surveys are typically about places you've visited, shopping experiences, or opinions on products. Most surveys take under 30 seconds.

Payments come as Google Play credits (Android) or PayPal cash (iOS). It's one of the most trusted survey apps due to Google's backing and the simplicity of the experience.

How It Works

Download the app and answer basic demographic questions. When surveys are available, you'll receive a notification. Surveys are often location-based – visit stores, restaurants, or tourist spots and you'll likely receive related surveys.

Each survey pays $0.10-$1.00, occasionally more. Credits expire after one year on Android, so spend them regularly on apps, games, books, or movies.

Earning Potential

Expect $5-30 per month depending on your activity and location. Urban users who visit many businesses tend to receive more surveys. It won't make you rich, but it's perfect for funding app purchases with zero effort.
