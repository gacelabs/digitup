What is PeoplePerHour?

PeoplePerHour (PPH) is a UK-based freelance marketplace that connects businesses with skilled professionals across writing, design, development, marketing, and business services. Founded in London in 2007, it has a strong European client base.

The platform combines elements of both Upwork (project bidding) and Fiverr (service packages called "Hourlies"). This hybrid approach gives freelancers flexibility in how they offer their services.

How It Works

Hourlies: Pre-packaged services with fixed prices – similar to Fiverr gigs. Create attractive offers and let buyers come to you.

Project Bids: Browse posted projects and submit proposals. Buyers review profiles and proposals before hiring.

PPH uses an AI matching system to suggest relevant projects and promote your profile to suitable buyers.

Earning Potential

UK and EU freelancers often earn £15-100+/hour depending on expertise. The platform handles payments in GBP, EUR, and USD. Service fees range from 3.5% to 20% depending on your earnings history with each client.

Pros

  • Strong UK and European client base
  • Lower competition than US platforms
  • Fees decrease with repeat clients
  • AI-powered project matching
  • Both hourly and project-based work
  • Escrow payment protection

Cons

  • Smaller project volume than Upwork
  • Monthly proposal limits on free accounts
  • Platform can feel dated
  • Customer support can be slow
  • Better for certain niches than others
