What is Survey Junkie?

Survey Junkie is one of the most popular and straightforward survey sites available. Unlike multi-reward platforms, Survey Junkie focuses purely on paid surveys – no watching videos, playing games, or other distractions. This simplicity makes it a favorite among serious survey takers.

With over 20 million members and an A rating from the BBB, Survey Junkie has built a solid reputation for reliable payments and quality surveys from major brands and research companies.

How It Works

After creating an account, you'll complete profile surveys that help match you with relevant opportunities. Surveys appear on your dashboard with estimated time and point values. Complete surveys honestly to earn points – 100 points equals $1.

The minimum cashout is just 500 points ($5), one of the lowest thresholds in the industry. Redeem for PayPal cash or gift cards.

Earning Potential

Most users earn $40-150 per month with consistent daily participation. Individual surveys pay 50-200 points ($0.50-$2.00) for 10-20 minutes. Higher-paying surveys ($5+) are available but less frequent.
