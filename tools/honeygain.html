What is Honeygain?

Honeygain is a passive income application that pays you for sharing your unused internet bandwidth. The app runs quietly in the background on your devices, allowing businesses to use your internet connection for web intelligence, content delivery, and market research purposes.

The concept is simple: most people don't use all their internet bandwidth. Honeygain allows you to monetize that excess capacity with zero effort beyond installing the app. It's one of the purest forms of passive income available online.

How It Works

Download and install Honeygain on your devices (Windows, Mac, Linux, Android, or iOS). The app automatically shares your unused bandwidth when your device is connected to the internet. You earn credits based on the amount of data shared – roughly 10GB = $1.

Honeygain's network is used by businesses for brand protection, price comparison, ad verification, and SEO monitoring. Your connection is never used for anything illegal, and your personal data is never accessed.

Earning Potential

Individual device earnings average $2-5/month depending on location and internet speed. Running Honeygain on multiple devices (up to 10 per account) can yield $20-50/month. Earnings are higher in the US, UK, and Western Europe.
