What is Creative Market?

Creative Market is a premium marketplace for design assets including fonts, graphics, templates, themes, photos, and 3D assets. It connects professional creators with businesses and designers who need high-quality resources for their projects.

Unlike general marketplaces, Creative Market caters specifically to the design community, meaning buyers expect and pay for quality. This makes it ideal for serious designers and illustrators.

What You Can Sell

Fonts: Original typefaces and font families

Graphics: Illustrations, icons, patterns, and textures

Templates: Resume templates, social media kits, presentation decks

Themes: WordPress, Shopify, and website templates

Photos & 3D: Stock photos and 3D models

Earning Potential

Sellers keep 60% of each sale (40% commission to Creative Market). Top sellers earn $5,000-50,000+/month, but success requires high-quality, professional work. Beginners might earn $100-500/month while building their portfolio.

Pros
