What is Freelancer.com?

Freelancer.com is one of the world's largest freelancing and crowdsourcing marketplaces, connecting over 60 million employers and freelancers globally. The platform supports a wide range of project types from website development and mobile apps to data entry and virtual assistance.

What sets Freelancer apart is its contest feature, where clients post design briefs and multiple freelancers submit entries competing for the prize. This gives newcomers a chance to showcase their skills even without an established reputation.

How It Works

Freelancers browse available projects and submit bids with their proposed price and timeline. Clients review bids, freelancer profiles, and portfolios before selecting who to hire. The platform supports both hourly and fixed-price projects with milestone-based payments.

Contests work differently â€“ clients post a brief with a prize amount, freelancers submit their work, and the client chooses a winner. If your entry isn't selected, you don't get paid, but winning contests builds your portfolio quickly.

Earning Potential

Entry-level freelancers typically earn $10-25/hour, while specialists in development, design, or marketing can command $50-150+/hour. Contest prizes range from $50 for simple logos to $1,000+ for comprehensive branding projects.

Pros

Cons
