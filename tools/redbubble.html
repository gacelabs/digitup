What is Redbubble?

Redbubble is a print-on-demand marketplace where artists upload designs that are printed on products like t-shirts, stickers, phone cases, mugs, and home decor. You create the art; Redbubble handles production, shipping, and customer service.

With zero upfront costs and no inventory requirements, it's an accessible way for artists and designers to monetize their creativity globally.

How It Works

Upload: Create an account and upload your artwork. Each design can be applied to 70+ product types automatically.

Set Margins: Choose your markup percentage above Redbubble's base price. Higher margins mean more profit but potentially fewer sales.

Earn: When someone buys a product with your design, Redbubble produces and ships it. You earn your margin on each sale.

Earning Potential

Earnings vary dramatically. Casual uploaders might earn $0-50/month. Dedicated artists with hundreds of designs and good SEO earn $500-5,000+/month. Stickers are often the best sellers due to low price points and impulse purchases.

Pros
