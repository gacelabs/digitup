What is Swagbucks?

Swagbucks is one of the most established rewards platforms, having paid out over $800 million to members since 2008. Unlike pure survey sites, Swagbucks offers multiple ways to earn – surveys, watching videos, online shopping cashback, web searches, playing games, and special offers.

Users earn "SB" (Swagbucks points) that can be redeemed for gift cards to popular retailers like Amazon, Walmart, Target, and PayPal cash. The versatility of earning methods makes Swagbucks one of the most user-friendly platforms for casual earners.

How to Earn on Swagbucks

Surveys (Answer): The primary earning method. Complete market research surveys ranging from 5-30 minutes. Earnings vary from 25-300+ SB per survey based on length and topic.

Shopping (Shop): Earn cashback on purchases from 1,500+ partner retailers. Simply click through Swagbucks before shopping to earn 1-10% back on eligible purchases.

Videos (Watch): Earn passive SB by watching video playlists. Lower earnings but requires minimal attention – great for running in the background.

Search (Search): Use Swagbucks as your default search engine and randomly earn SB on searches. Not every search pays, but it adds up over time.

Offers (Discover): Sign up for free trials, download apps, or complete sponsored offers. Some high-value offers pay 1,000+ SB.

Earning Potential

Active users typically earn $50-200 per month depending on time invested. The key is using multiple earning methods rather than relying solely on surveys. Power users who maximize daily goals and special bonuses can earn more.

Maximizing Your Earnings

1. Complete Your Profile: Fill out all profile surveys to qualify for more opportunities. Incomplete profiles receive fewer survey invitations.

2. Hit Daily Goals: Swagbucks offers daily earning goals with bonus SB. Consistently hitting goals adds 10-15% to your monthly earnings.

3. Use SwagButton: Install the browser extension to automatically find cashback opportunities while shopping and get notified of bonus earning opportunities.

4. Check Swag Codes: Follow Swagbucks on social media for free Swag Codes that give instant SB when redeemed.
