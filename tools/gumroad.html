What is Gumroad?

Gumroad is a platform for creators to sell digital products directly to their audience. It's popular among writers, artists, musicians, educators, and software developers for selling ebooks, courses, music, art, fonts, templates, and software.

Unlike marketplaces that bury your products among competitors, Gumroad gives you a direct storefront. You bring your audience; Gumroad handles payments, file delivery, and basic marketing tools.

How It Works

Upload: Add your digital products with descriptions, pricing, and preview images.

Share: Get a unique link to share with your audience via social media, email, or your website.

Sell: Customers purchase and receive instant access to downloads. Gumroad handles payment processing and delivery.

Earning Potential

Earnings are unlimited and depend entirely on your audience and product quality. Some creators earn $100/month while others make $100,000+/month. The platform works best for creators with existing audiences from social media, newsletters, or blogs.

Pros

  • Creator-friendly platform
  • No monthly fees (pay per sale)
  • Simple, clean interface
  • Supports subscriptions/memberships
  • Built-in email marketing
  • Global payments supported

Cons

  • 10% fee on sales (flat rate)
  • No built-in marketplace/discovery
  • Must bring your own audience
  • Limited customization options
  • Customer service is basic
