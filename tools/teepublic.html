What is TeePublic?

TeePublic is a print-on-demand marketplace known for its artist-friendly approach and higher royalties compared to competitors. Upload your designs, and TeePublic prints them on t-shirts, hoodies, stickers, phone cases, and home goods when orders come in.

The platform is particularly popular for pop culture-inspired designs, fanart, and niche humor. Its community-focused approach and regular sales events help drive traffic to artist stores.

How It Works

Create a free account, upload your artwork, and TeePublic automatically applies it to their product catalog. When someone buys, TeePublic handles production, shipping, and customer service. You earn a fixed royalty per product sold.

Earning Potential

Royalties are fixed: $4 per t-shirt, $2 per sticker, with varying rates for other products. During TeePublic's frequent sales (when products are discounted), royalties drop but volume increases. Consistent uploaders with 100+ designs earn $50-2,000+/month.

Pros

  • Higher royalties than Redbubble
  • Strong built-in traffic
  • Active community
  • Free to join and use
  • Regular promotional sales
  • Good for pop culture niches

Cons

  • No control over pricing
  • Reduced royalties during sales
  • Fewer product options than competitors
  • $25 minimum payout
  • High competition in popular niches
