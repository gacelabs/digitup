What is Prolific?

Prolific is an academic research platform that connects participants with researchers from universities and institutions worldwide. Unlike commercial survey sites, Prolific focuses on ethical treatment of participants with fair pay rates and interesting studies.

The platform is known for having the highest pay rates in the survey industry, with a minimum of $8/hour (equivalent). Studies range from psychology experiments to linguistics research to product testing.

Why Prolific Pays More

Prolific requires researchers to pay at least £6/hour ($8/hour) and recommends £9/hour. This ethical pay policy means you earn significantly more per hour than on commercial survey sites. Studies typically show the estimated completion time and total pay upfront.

Studies are often more engaging than typical surveys – you might play games, watch videos, or participate in interesting experiments rather than answering repetitive questions about brands.

Earning Potential

Active participants earn $60-200+ per month. Study availability depends on your demographics – some profiles receive more studies than others. The effective hourly rate of $8-15/hour makes Prolific one of the best-paying survey platforms available.
