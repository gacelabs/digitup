What is Pinecone Research?

Pinecone Research is a premium, invite-only market research panel operated by Nielsen (a leading global data company). Known for consistent $3 payments per survey and occasional product testing opportunities, it's considered one of the most desirable survey panels to join.

Unlike open survey sites, Pinecone carefully controls its membership to maintain data quality. This exclusivity means less competition for surveys and more consistent opportunities for members.

How to Join

Pinecone Research is invite-only, but invitations appear periodically through banner ads, email campaigns, and partner websites. When enrollment is open, you'll need to complete a demographic screener to see if they need participants matching your profile.

Once accepted, you'll receive survey invitations via email. Each survey pays a flat $3 (300 points) regardless of length. Some members also receive products to test at home and provide feedback.

Earning Potential

Members typically receive 2-4 surveys per month, earning $30-90/month. Product testing opportunities (when available) may include keeping the product. The consistent $3/survey rate makes earnings predictable.
