What is Remotasks?

Remotasks (owned by Scale AI) is a platform specializing in AI training data, particularly for autonomous vehicles, robotics, and computer vision. Workers ("Taskers") complete data labeling tasks that help train machine learning models.

The platform is known for its free training courses that teach you skills while qualifying you for higher-paying tasks. It's popular in developing countries but available worldwide.

Types of Tasks

Image Annotation: Draw boxes, polygons, or keypoints around objects in images for computer vision training.

3D Point Cloud (LiDAR): Label objects in 3D sensor data from autonomous vehicles. Higher-paying but requires training.

Text & Audio: Transcription, categorization, and content tasks.

Video Annotation: Track objects across video frames.

Earning Potential

Earnings vary widely: basic tasks pay $1-5/hour, while specialized LiDAR work can reach $10-15/hour. Top performers with advanced skills earn $200-800/month. Success depends on speed, accuracy, and qualifying for premium tasks.
