What is UserTesting?

UserTesting is a platform that pays people to test websites, apps, and prototypes. Companies use this feedback to improve their user experience before launching products. As a tester, you'll record your screen and voice while completing tasks and sharing your thoughts.

It's one of the highest-paying micro-task opportunities, with standard tests paying $10 for about 20 minutes of work. That's an effective rate of $30/hour â€“ significantly better than surveys or typical micro-tasks.

How It Works

After signing up and completing a practice test, you'll receive notifications when tests matching your profile are available. You'll be given a scenario (like "You want to buy a gift for a friend") and asked to complete tasks while speaking your thoughts aloud.

Tests are recorded for review by the client. Your feedback helps companies understand real user behavior and identify usability issues.

Earning Potential

Standard tests: $10 for 20 minutes. Live conversations: $30-120 for 30-60 minutes. Active testers complete 3-10 tests per week, earning $100-400/month. Test availability depends heavily on your demographic profile.
