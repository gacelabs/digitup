What is Clickworker?

Clickworker is a German-based crowdsourcing platform that connects businesses with a global workforce for AI training data, content creation, and data processing tasks. With over 4.5 million registered workers worldwide, it's one of the largest micro-task platforms outside of Amazon MTurk.

The platform specializes in tasks that help train AI systems: text creation and editing, image and video annotation, data categorization, search engine evaluation, and surveys.

Types of Tasks

UHRS (Universal Human Relevance System): Microsoft's crowdsourcing platform accessed through Clickworker. Includes search result rating, query relevance, and content moderation. Often the highest-paying tasks.

Text Creation: Write product descriptions, articles, or SEO content. Pay varies by word count and complexity.

Data Categorization: Sort products, tag images, or classify content into categories.

Surveys: Short questionnaires similar to traditional survey sites.

Earning Potential

Earnings range from $50-300/month for casual workers to $500-1,000+ for dedicated users who qualify for premium tasks. UHRS tasks typically offer the best hourly rates ($8-15/hour). Task availability fluctuates significantly.
