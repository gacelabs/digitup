What is FlexJobs?

FlexJobs is a premium job board specializing in remote, work-from-home, and flexible job opportunities. Unlike free job boards filled with scams and MLM schemes, FlexJobs hand-screens every listing to ensure legitimacy.

The platform requires a subscription to access job listings, but this fee funds the research team that vets every opportunity. For job seekers tired of filtering through scams, FlexJobs provides peace of mind.

What Makes FlexJobs Different

100% Hand-Screened: Every job is researched and verified by FlexJobs staff. No scams, no MLMs, no "too good to be true" postings.

Quality Employers: Jobs from companies like Apple, Amazon, UnitedHealth, and thousands of other legitimate employers.

Job Types: Full-time, part-time, freelance, and employee positions across 50+ career categories.

Earning Potential

FlexJobs lists real jobs with real salaries – from entry-level at $30,000/year to executive positions at $150,000+. Freelance opportunities vary by field. The subscription cost ($9.95-24.95/month) is minimal compared to the salary of legitimate positions found.
