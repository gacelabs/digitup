What is 99designs?

99designs is the world's largest graphic design marketplace, connecting businesses with talented designers for logos, websites, packaging, and more. The platform pioneered the design contest model where multiple designers compete for a prize.

Now part of Vistaprint, 99designs offers both contest-based work and direct 1-to-1 projects. It's particularly popular for logo design, brand identity, and marketing materials.

How to Earn on 99designs

Design Contests: Clients post briefs with prize amounts ($299-$1,599 for logos). Designers submit entries, clients provide feedback, and winners receive the prize. Popular but competitive â€“ no payment unless you win.

1-to-1 Projects: Build a portfolio and get hired directly by clients. Set your own rates and work without competition. Better for established designers with strong reviews.

Earning Potential

Logo contest prizes range from $299-$1,599. Complete branding packages can reach $2,000+. Direct projects allow custom pricing, with experienced designers charging $500-5,000+ per project.
