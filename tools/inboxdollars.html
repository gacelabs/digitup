What is InboxDollars?

InboxDollars is a rewards platform that pays real cash (not points) for completing surveys, reading emails, watching videos, playing games, and shopping online. Founded in 2000, it has paid out over $80 million to members.

The platform is known for its $5 sign-up bonus and straightforward cash balance system – no converting points to dollars or figuring out redemption rates.

Ways to Earn

Surveys: The highest-paying activity. Surveys range from $0.25 to $5+ depending on length and topic.

PaidEmails: Get paid $0.02-0.10 just for reading promotional emails – takes seconds.

Videos: Watch video playlists for small amounts – good for passive earning.

Shopping: Earn cashback at partner retailers when you shop online.

Earning Potential

Active users typically earn $30-100 per month. The $5 sign-up bonus helps you reach the $15 minimum payout faster. Payment is via check or prepaid Visa.
