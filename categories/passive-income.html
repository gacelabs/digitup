Passive Income Platforms

Set it and forget it. Earn money with minimal ongoing effort.

Earn While You Sleep

Passive income represents the holy grail of online earning – money that comes in with little to no ongoing effort after initial setup. While truly passive income typically requires significant upfront investment (time or money), the platforms below offer low-barrier ways to generate supplementary income passively.

These platforms work in the background while you go about your daily life. Bandwidth-sharing apps use your unused internet connection, cashback programs reward your regular shopping, and certain apps pay you simply for having them installed on your devices.

Realistic expectations: Individual passive income streams are modest – typically $10-50 per month each. However, stacking multiple passive income sources can create meaningful supplementary income over time. The key is setting them up once and letting them run.
